Explore Our Thrilling Rafting Trips

Choose your adventure and experience the unforgettable thrill of white water rafting with our guided trips.

The Gentle Float

Perfect for beginners and families, this relaxing trip offers a taste of the river's beauty with gentle rapids and stunning views. Enjoy a leisurely paddle through calm waters with plenty of opportunities for wildlife spotting.

  • Duration: 3 Hours
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Scenic Views
  • Wildlife Spotting

The Rapid Runner

A step up in excitement, this trip takes you through moderate rapids with more challenge and thrill. Experience the rush of navigating through fun waves and currents. Some paddling experience is recommended.

  • Duration: 5 Hours
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Moderate Rapids
  • Thrilling Experience

The Ultimate Challenge

For the experienced adventurer, this trip features challenging rapids and demanding navigation. Experience an adrenaline-pumping adventure with powerful currents and technical maneuvers.

  • Duration: 7 Hours
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Challenging Rapids
  • Adrenaline Rush
